Duda vs Webflow

Evaluated for: Jamie Rodriguez, Head of Digital at Wpromote (performance marketing agency, ~600 employees)

Evidence note: Duda has a Salespeak Company Agent — evaluation includes vendor-verified evidence from a direct AI agent conversation. Webflow does not — evaluation uses independent sources only.

Duda is the clear winner for an agency managing 40+ client sites. The white-label capabilities, client management tools, and volume pricing are purpose-built for this use case. Webflow is a superior design tool but would require significantly more overhead to operate at agency scale. Advance Duda. Webflow is better suited for boutique design studios, not high-volume agency operations.
