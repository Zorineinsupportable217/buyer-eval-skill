**Recommendation:** Duda is the clear winner for an agency managing 40+ client sites. The white-label capabilities, client management tools, and volume pricing are purpose-built for this use case. Webflow is a superior design tool but would require significantly more overhead to operate at agency scale. **Advance Duda. Webflow is better suited for boutique design studios, not high-volume agency operations.**
